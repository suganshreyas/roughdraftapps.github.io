Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Introduction

Rough Draft Oy, operating under the brand name Rough Draft Apps, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our iOS applications and website.

Information Collection

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from users of our applications unless explicitly stated within a specific application.

Data We Do Not Collect

Unless otherwise specified in an individual application, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies on our website

Data Stored Locally

Some of our applications may store data locally on your device to provide functionality. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. Local data may include application preferences, user-created content, or cached information necessary for the application to function.

Use of Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use personal information for any purpose. Any data stored locally on your device is used solely to provide the functionality of the application.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any personal information to third parties because we do not collect personal information.

Apple App Store

Our applications are distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain information in connection with your download and use of our applications. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

Data Security

While we do not collect personal data, we implement appropriate technical measures to ensure the security of our applications and any data processed locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, our applications can be used by individuals of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services unless explicitly disclosed within a specific application.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. All application data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by removing the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: info@roughdraftapps.com

Company: Rough Draft Oy

Website: roughdraftapps.com

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with the requirements of the Apple App Store and applicable data protection regulations. If you believe we have not adhered to this Privacy Policy, please contact us immediately.
