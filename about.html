About

Rough Draft Apps is an independent iOS studio based on a simple idea: meaningful software is rarely finished — it’s revised.

The name reflects how the work is approached. Start small. Ship early. Pay attention. Improve what matters.

The studio focuses on utility-driven apps — tools that quietly fit into daily life and earn their place through usefulness rather than novelty.

Rough Draft Apps is operated as a solo practice, valuing clarity, restraint, and long-term care over rapid expansion.
