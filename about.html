About Rough Draft Apps

Independent iOS development

Company Information

Rough Draft Apps is the brand name under which Rough Draft Oy operates its iOS application development business. We are an independent software development company registered in Finland.

What We Do

We specialize in developing iOS applications for iPhone and iPad. Our work encompasses the complete application lifecycle, including concept development, design, implementation, testing, and ongoing support and updates.

Platform Focus

We develop exclusively for Apple's iOS platform, ensuring our applications take full advantage of the features and capabilities of iPhone and iPad devices. All of our applications are distributed through the Apple App Store.

Development Approach

Our approach emphasizes creating reliable, well-designed applications that provide genuine value to users. We follow Apple's design guidelines and best practices to ensure our applications meet high standards for quality and usability.
