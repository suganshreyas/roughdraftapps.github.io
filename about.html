About

Rough Draft Apps is an independent iOS studio based on a simple idea: Done is better than perfect. Shipping something simple, and value-additive to users, is more important than chasing perfection.

Here to ship tools that quietly fit into daily life of users. Rough Draft Apps is operated as a solo practice, valuing clarity, restraint, and long-term care over rapid expansion.
