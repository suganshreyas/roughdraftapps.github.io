About

Rough Draft Apps is a small iOS studio focused on building and refining simple ideas.

The name comes from a belief that software is a process. Every product begins imperfect, shaped gradually by real use, feedback, and careful iteration.

We care about:

  • Clarity over cleverness
  • Progress over polish
  • Tools that respect attention and privacy

Rough Draft Apps is based in Finland and builds apps for Apple platforms.
